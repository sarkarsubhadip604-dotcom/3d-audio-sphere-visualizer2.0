How to Use

  • Upload an audio file (MP3, WAV, OGG, or M4A format)
  • Click the play button to start audio playback
  • Watch the 3D sphere react to your music in real-time
  • Use your mouse to rotate and zoom the 3D view
  • Adjust volume and sensitivity for the best experience

Browser Requirements:

This visualizer requires a modern browser with Web Audio API and WebGL support. Works best in Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge.
